About Me

Hi! I’m Sam, an inquisitive UI
designer who loves UX. I create clean & engaging designs.

I thrive in collaborative environments where I can look at the big picture and then dive headfirst into the details; I live for great critique and feedback.

Before venturing to Chicago to learn UI, I helped budding code-dabblers become bona fide developers at Hack Reactor and learned some code basics along the way. Before that, I spent two years at UserTesting evangelizing UX to small and medium businesses to help make the web a more user-friendly place.

When I’m not thinking about my next design, you can find me hiking through the redwoods with my French Bulldog, Max, traveling to sunny places like Tulum, or doodling in sketchbooks at Lake Merritt. I’m always excited to chat about design, dog parenting, art, or whatever else. Connect with me on LinkedIn or, even better, say hi!
